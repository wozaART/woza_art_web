# Vern Fables - Comic Book Release

## Event Description

GREETINGS AND SALUTATIONS!

You are cordially invited to the **Vern Fables** - Comic Book - Art Exhibition of WB Pretorius and the launch of his two self-published comics.

* Vern Fables 1 - Gothic Legends
* Vern Fables 2- Chronicles of the Galaxy Gops

The exhibition will open on 21 November 2025 at <Link href="https://www.facebook.com/TrentGallery">Trent Gallery</Link>, located at 198 Long Street, Waterkloof, Pretoria. It runs until 28 November 2025.

## About Trent Gallery

Trent Gallery is a hidden gem for artist enthusiasts nestled in Waterkloof, Pretoria. Stuart Trent is extreme helpful and knowledgeable and you can find curated works of significance at this gallery at any given time.
