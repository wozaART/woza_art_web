# Azibuyele Emasisweni

## Event Description

The South African Gallery of Legends, in proud partnership with Ditsong Museums of South Africa, invites you to experience a landmark cultural event

An award-winning exhibition by legendary artist Prof. Pitika Ntuli, featuring 45 evocative bone sculptures that speak to African spirituality, ancestry, and healing. Ntuli's powerful work earned global acclaim, winning the People's Choice "You-2" Award at the 7th Annual Global Fine Art Awards in Paris, 2021.

* **Open to the public:**<br/>
1 November 2025 - 30 April 2026

* **Venue:**<br/>
Ditsong National Museum of Cultural History - Pretoria

Come witness the return to our roots through art that transcends time.
