## About Tina Skukan Gallery

The Tina Skukan Art Gallery is situated on one of the last remaining smallholdings in the suburb of Faerie Glen, Pretoria, South Africa, which originated out of the former Valley Farm smallholdings. This incredible landmark secretly tucked away in the heart of suburbia forms part of the Bronberg Nature Reserve.
