## About Johann van Heerden's Art Gallery

Johann van Heerden's is a contemporary art gallery featuring multiple new exhibitions every single month throughout the year.

There are up to 6 exhibition openings on the 1st Sunday of the month at 11 am. These events are usually a popular spot for local and regional art enthusiasts. There have been several prominent South African artists showcased here. The gallery also works hard to foster an inclusive environment for new upcoming artists.

**Opening Times:**

* Tuesday to Thursday (9am - 4pm)
* Friday to Saturday (9am - 3pm)
* Sunday (9am - 2pm)
* Monday (Closed)
